## Understanding Your Progress

### Practice Count
- Shows how many times you've practiced each word
- Higher count = more practice (but not necessarily better score!)
- Use to identify which words you've been practicing

### Average Score
- Calculated from all your practice attempts for that word
- Updates automatically after each practice session
- Color-coded for quick visual feedback:
  - **Green**: You've mastered this word! Keep it up.
  - **Light Green**: Good pronunciation, minor improvements possible
  - **Amber**: Fair pronunciation, needs more practice
  - **Red**: Needs significant improvement, practice more

### No Statistics Yet?
If you haven't practiced a word yet, you'll only see the word information without practice count or average score. Start practicing to build your statistics!

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working
1. Check app permissions in device settings
2. Ensure no other app is using the microphone
3. Restart the app
4. Check that microphone is not blocked or covered

### Poor Recognition Accuracy
1. Speak more slowly and clearly
2. Reduce background noise
3. Hold device closer (but not too close)
4. Make sure you're speaking English, not Vietnamese
5. Try practicing with easier words first

### No Vocabulary to Practice
If you see "Ch∆∞a c√≥ t·ª´ v·ª±ng" (No vocabulary yet):
1. Go to "üîç Tra t·ª´" (Search) to look up new words

### Scores Seem Inaccurate
- AI scoring is approximate and focuses on general pronunciation
- Some words may be harder to recognize than others
- Multiple practices will give you a better average
- Use feedback to understand specific mistakes
